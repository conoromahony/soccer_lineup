Frequently Asked Questions

Once you set up your team, you can generate lineups. For each game, you indicate which of your players are present, who is playing in goal, and we figure out the lineups to try to get all outfield player as close as possible to equal playing time.

No, but we get it as close as possible. Sometimes it's not practical to get exactly equal playing time. For instance, to get exactly equal playing time may require so many substitutions that players would not be able to get into any "flow" while playing. In such cases, we balance the need for equal playing time with the need to have a "reasonable" substitution strategy.

Players who volunteer to play some of the game in goal will end up with more playing time that players who only play in outfield positions. We take this approach to both express appreciation for those players who play in goal, and to acknowledge that often playing in goal is less physically demanding than playing in the outfield positions.
