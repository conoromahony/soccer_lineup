Frequently Asked Questions

Once you set up your team, you can generate lineups. For eah game, you indicate which of your players are present, who is playing in goal, and we figure out the lineups to ensure all outfield player get equal playing time.

Players who volunteer to play some of the game in goal will end up with more playing time that players who only play in outfield positions. We take this approach to both express appreciation for those players who volunteer to play in goal, as well as to acknowledge that often playing in goal is less "involved" than playing in the outfield positions.
